☀️ What is ShineTwoPlay?

ShineTwoPlay is a free, open-source browser-based platform for playing 2-player online games with friends. No downloads, no sign-ups — just create a room, share the code, and start playing!

With 16 multiplayer games, real-time voice chat, image sharing, and a beautiful interface, ShineTwoPlay makes playing with a friend feel effortless and fun.

🔒 Privacy Policy

🚫
No Account Required. We don't collect emails, passwords, or any personal information. Your username is temporary and only used during a game session.
💾
No Permanent Data Storage. Room data, messages, and chat history are stored temporarily in Redis and automatically deleted when the room expires (typically within a few hours).
🎤
Microphone Access. Voice calling and voice messages require microphone permission. Audio is transmitted peer-to-peer via WebRTC and is never stored on our servers.
📷
Shared Images. Images sent in chat are temporarily stored on the server for the duration of the room session and are automatically cleaned up afterward.
🍪
Cookies & Local Storage. We use browser localStorage to remember your username and gender preference. No tracking cookies are used.
📊
No Analytics or Tracking. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking services.
